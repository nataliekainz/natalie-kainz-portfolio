About
Natalie Kainz

I'm a multimedia journalist currently working as an Associate Producer at NBC News' Top Story with Tom Llamas. I graduated from Yale University with honors in Political Science and am based in New York City.

My reporting experience is split across my hometown Hong Kong and my current home in the United States. I've worked for CNN International Productions, NBC News, The South China Morning Post, and the New Haven Independent.

I was the Multimedia Managing Editor of the Yale Daily News from 2021–2022, and was a scholar of the Yale Journalism Initiative and winner of the 1912 Yale News Board Prize for reporting in 2023.

I'm passionate about reporting on international affairs, culture, and technology.
